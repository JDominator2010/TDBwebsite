Quick Info

Where are we from?
We are from Northwest High School, High Ridge, Missouri
As a team, what do we strive for?
As a team, we strive to innovate and create a fully functioning robot that can achieve our goals. We also intend to stand out and make a name for ourselves.
What is the highest level competition we've been to?
The highest level competition we have ever made it to is Worlds, which we made it to in our first year. Otherwise, we have been to U.S Open multipe times.
What is our robots name, and what does it mean?
Our robots name is Trogdor, this is from a old meme on the internet back in 2001. The meme comes from a website called homestarrunner.com but the site is not around anymore. But one of the memes was the character Strongbad reading emails, and one was to draw a dragon, and he drew Trogdor.
